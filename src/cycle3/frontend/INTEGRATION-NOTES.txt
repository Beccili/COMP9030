Integration Notes (Frontend â†’ PHP API)
-----------------------------------
1) Place these files in your frontend root:
   - api.js
   - register.js   (replaces your old register.js)
   - artwork-submit.js (new; referenced by artwork-submit.html)

2) Ensure your pages include the scripts as ES modules, e.g.:
   <script>window.API_BASE = "http://localhost/backend";</script>
   <script type="module" src="api.js"></script>
   <script type="module" src="register.js"></script>
   or
   <script type="module" src="artwork-submit.js"></script>

3) Endpoints used follow README:
   - POST /auth.php?action=register
   - POST /auth.php?action=login
   - GET  /auth.php?action=verify
   - GET  /artworks.php
   - GET  /artworks.php?id={id}
   - POST /artworks.php?session_id={...}

4) Save session_id after login:
   localStorage.setItem("IAA_session_id", sessionId)

5) CORS: If frontend and backend are on different origins, set proper CORS headers in PHP.
