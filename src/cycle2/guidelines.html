About Our Guidelines

Our usage guidelines have been developed in consultation with Indigenous communities, cultural experts, and Traditional Owners to ensure respectful and appropriate engagement with Indigenous art and cultural heritage materials.

These guidelines reflect our commitment to cultural protocol, educational integrity, and the protection of sensitive cultural information. They serve as a framework for both content creators and platform users to engage meaningfully and respectfully with Indigenous cultural heritage.
