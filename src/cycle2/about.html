About Indigenous Art Atlas

Our mission is to respectfully document and share Indigenous art across Australia, fostering cultural understanding and appreciation while maintaining appropriate cultural protocols.

Our Mission

The Indigenous Art Atlas exists to celebrate and preserve the rich cultural heritage of Indigenous peoples across Australia. We provide a digital platform that connects communities, artists, researchers, and art enthusiasts while respecting Traditional Knowledge and cultural protocols.

Every artwork in our collection is documented with care, ensuring proper cultural context, attribution, and respect for the stories and traditions they represent. We work directly with Indigenous communities and artists to ensure authentic representation.
