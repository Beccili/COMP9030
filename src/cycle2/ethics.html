About Our Ethics

The Indigenous Art Atlas is built upon a foundation of cultural respect, ethical documentation, and community collaboration. We recognize that Indigenous art represents profound cultural knowledge, spiritual significance, and thousands of years of continuous cultural practice.

Our ethical framework ensures that all artwork documentation is conducted with appropriate community consultation, proper attribution, and respect for cultural protocols. We are committed to transparency, accountability, and the ongoing education of our team and users.
